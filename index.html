Research & Engineering Interests

Areas I am most interested in, both theoretically and practically.

My work focuses on the modeling, optimization, and control of renewable energy systems, especially airborne wind energy and related power system integration problems.

I enjoy problems that combine rigorous mathematical formulations with practical engineering constraints, from farm layout optimization to operational strategies and system-level integration.
