Research & Engineering Interests

My work focuses on the modeling, optimization and control of renewable energy systems, especially airborne wind energy farms and their integration into power systems.

I enjoy problems that require rigorous mathematical formulations while respecting practical engineering constraints: farm layout design, power smoothing strategies, energy storage sizing and system-level integration.
